Customer Journey Map

'Tech-Savvy Theresa's' Morning Coffee

1. Awareness (Waking Up)

Actions
  • Alarm goes off.
  • Hits snooze. Checks phone.
Thoughts
  • "Ugh, I'm so tired."
  • "I *need* coffee for my 9 AM meeting."
Feelings
Groggy, Anxious, Low-energy

2. Preparation (Making Coffee)

Actions
  • Grinds beans, boils kettle.
  • Pours water, waits.
  • Adds milk.
Thoughts
  • "This is taking forever."
  • "I hope this tastes okay. I'm running late."
Feelings
Impatient, Rushed, Stressed

3. Transition (Moving to Desk)

Actions
  • Walks to home office.
  • Sits down, logs in.
Thoughts
  • "Okay, don't spill."
  • "Finally. Ah, that's better."
Feelings
Relief, Comfort, Focus Building

4. Consumption (The Interruption)

Actions
  • Takes one perfect sip.
  • 'Slack' notification.
  • Joins a 45-min unscheduled call.
Thoughts
  • "Oh, come on! I just made this!"
  • "...and, it's lukewarm. Gross."
  • "My morning is ruined."
Feelings
Frustrated, Angry, Disappointed

5. "Solving" the Problem

Actions
  • Carries mug to kitchen.
  • Dumps half of it out.
  • Microwaves the rest.
Thoughts
  • "This will taste like rubber."
  • "What a waste of good beans."
Feelings
Defeated, Resigned, Unsatisfied

6. Post-Consumption

Actions
  • Finishes the bad coffee.
  • Puts the mug in the sink.
Thoughts
  • "Now I have to wash this. Great."
Feelings
Annoyed
Emotional High
Neutral / Annoyed
Negative Emotion
Key Pain Point / Emotional Low
